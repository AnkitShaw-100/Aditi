Brand Statement

ADITI is not defence news. It is strategic thinking for India.

The magazine examines the paradoxes behind power: why countries acquire capability but fail to integrate it, why doctrine lags technology, why geography still bends ambition, and why India's strategic culture must be argued with seriousness rather than performed for attention.

The ADITI Framework

Most defence media catalogues capability. ADITI interrogates choice.

A

Armament

The means of war: weapons, platforms, logistics, industrial capacity, supply chains, and sustainment.

Pillar / A

D

Doctrine

The ideas of war: how force is conceived, organised, and employed to achieve political ends.

Pillar / D

I

Initiative

Agency under uncertainty: who sets the agenda, controls escalation, and dictates tempo.

Pillar / I

T

Terrain

The environment of conflict: physical, political, technological, informational, legal, or cognitive.

Pillar / T

I

Integration

The joining of parts: civil-military fusion, inter-service jointness, and whole-of-nation effort.

Pillar / I
